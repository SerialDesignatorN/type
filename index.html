Redo: Ctrl+Y (Cmd+Y)

Print: Ctrl+P (Cmd+P)

Delete: Del

Bold: Ctrl+B (Cmd+B)

Italic: Ctrl+I (Cmd+I)

Underline: Ctrl+U (Cmd+U)
